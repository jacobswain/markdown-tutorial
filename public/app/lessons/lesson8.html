Inline Hyperlinks

Markdown supports two different types of hyperlinks--inline links and reference links.

To create an inline link, wrap the link's text in brackets [] and the URL in parenthesis (). 


    [Google](http://www.google.com)

You can also add a title attribute to your hyperlink after providing a URL. 


    [Google](http://www.google.com "Title")

Example
